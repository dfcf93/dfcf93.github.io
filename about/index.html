About

I am a technologist and tinkerer currently located in Urbana, Illinois pursing a PhD in CS at UIUC under Prof. Heng Ji as part of the Blender Lab.

My primary interests lie in the intersection of efficient models, natural language processing and information understanding systems(IR, IE, etc.)

I was born and raised in Mexico City, got a BS in CS at RPI, MS in Computational Linguistics (CS focused NLP) at UW and have worked at many tech firms along the way.

Outside of work my family and I make wine, ferment food, surf, kiteboard, camp, and renovate houses.
