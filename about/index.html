About

I am a technologist and tinkerer currently located in Urbana, Illinois

I am pursing a PhD in Computer Science at UIUC where I will work under Prof. Heng Ji as part of the Blender Lab.

I am researching how to make NLP and transformer models more efficient with the good folks at Neural Magic.

My primary interests lie in the intersection of efficient models, natural language processing and information understanding systems(IR, IE, etc.)

I was born and raised in Mexico City attended ASF

I have a BS in Computer Science from RPI(2011-2015) and a MS in Computational Linguistics from the University of Washington(2018-2020).

Along the way I worked for KIO Networks,GE,Cisco,Basis Tech, and Microsoft where I gradually dug my Computer Science hole deeper.

Outside of work and schooling I have a beautiful wife (Zoe), an adorable todler (Theo). Together we make wine, ferment food, surf, kiteboard, camp, and renovate houses.
