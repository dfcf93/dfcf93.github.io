Quite Francly

Status:Consumed

Year: 2018

Tasting Notes:Oak, lilac, pepper, plum

Grape Origin:[Eclectic Vineyard](https://www.eclecticvineyards.com)

Varietal:Cabernet Franc

Production:50 Bottles

In 2018 we decided to go all in on Cab Franc and it did not dissapoint. The grapes matured beautifully this year and came in ~27 Brix when we picked! Wine was fermented naturally after 4 days of cold soak. Fermentation on skin went for 5 months and then only the free run was placed into new american oak barrel.
